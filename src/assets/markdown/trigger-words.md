# Trigger words

Outstanding communication is the basis of humans living together happily. Great dialogue with empathy and fun encourage collaboration and future efforts.

On certain events, negative feedback may be appropriate as long as an individual recipient is not targeted in public. Without any honest response we cannot self-improve. So, bad criticism has to happen sometimes. Applies at all times: It's the sound that makes the music. In addition, always remember the [four-sides model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Four-sides_model) of communication in this regard.

To sum things up, using any of those words may lead a discourse to go down the drain rather than develop:

* any personal insults
* any political terms ending with -ism, e.g. capitalism, socialism, you name it
* any references to WWII era
* any religious terms
* any rhetorical misuse
* any steering against humanity and the good will

Don't be a "Bad Guy" and do not use these words in real-time conversations. We all want to live together peacefully after all, don't we?
